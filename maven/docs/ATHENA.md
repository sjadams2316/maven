## Executive Summary

**Athena** is Maven's intelligence orchestration layer â€” a hybrid system that routes every query to the optimal combination of AI models and data sources, then synthesizes them into a single, confident answer.

**The insight:** No single AI is best at everything. Athena combines:
- **Centralized models** for speed and reasoning (Groq, Claude, Perplexity)
- **Decentralized Bittensor subnets** for signals and cost (Chutes, Vanta, Desearch)

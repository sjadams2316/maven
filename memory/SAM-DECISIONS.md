## Business Strategy

### Maven is RIA-First (2026-02-05)
**Decision:** Build our own RIA first, not SaaS for other advisors.
**Reasoning:** 
- Eat our own cooking
- Keep best tech in-house
- Word of mouth > paid acquisition
- "Clients don't want service. They want an experience."

### Jon is the Tip of the Spear (2026-02-09)
**Decision:** Jon does the breakaway first. Sam joins when traction is proven.
**Reasoning:**
- Sam has $720K job, family to support
- Jon has existing client relationships
- Validates before Sam takes income hit
- Reduces family risk

### Two Products, One Platform (2026-02-05)
**Decision:** Maven Pro (for RIAs) + Maven Personal (direct to consumer)
**Reasoning:**
- RIA validates with sophisticated users
- Consumer is larger market long-term
- Same core intelligence powers both

## Technical Decisions

### Project Pantheon - Multi-Agent Architecture (2026-02-09)
**Decision:** Build Maven with specialized agent teams working in parallel.
**Reasoning:**
- Exponential growth through parallelization
- Specialization creates depth
- Compound learning 24/7
- Sam can't direct everything manually

### AI-Native, Not AI-Assisted (2026-02-05)
**Decision:** Maven IS the intelligence, not a dashboard with AI features.
**Reasoning:**
- Legacy players are bolting on AI
- We're building from first principles
- This is the moat
